Executive Summary

Project Overview

Saveetha Hub represents a comprehensive academic success platform designed specifically for Saveetha University students. The platform serves as a centralized hub for academic tools, resources, and community engagement, positioning itself as an essential digital companion for student academic journey.

With its tagline "Your Academic Success Hub," the platform demonstrates a clear vision to provide integrated solutions for CGPA calculation, attendance tracking, learning resources, and career development opportunities.

Key Findings

  • Comprehensive feature set addressing core academic needs
  • Strong integration with Saveetha University ecosystem
  • User interface requires modernization and optimization
  • Significant potential for mobile app development and AI integration
